Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At ADBWorks, we believe in absolute privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in our mobile applications for iOS.

1. Data Collection & Privacy

At ADBWORKS LLC, we believe that your personal data belongs to you. Our mobile applications are built from the ground up to protect user privacy. We do not collect, harvest, or store any of your personal information on our own servers. Any data or settings you create in the app are stored locally on your device and, if enabled, synced securely to your personal Apple iCloud account. We have no access to this data.

2. In-App Purchases & Payment Processing

Our apps use Apple's In-App Purchase system for premium features. We do not process or store any financial data or credit card details. All transactions are securely handled by Apple subject to the App Store's privacy policy.

3. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

Because we do not collect, process, or store any personal data, there is no personal data of yours for us to access, rectify, port, or erase under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). However, if you contact us via email for support, we will only use your email address to assist you and will delete the conversation once resolved.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please feel free to reach out to us at support@adbworks.com.
